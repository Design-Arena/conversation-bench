
# Atlas Personal Assistant

## Overview
Atlas Personal Assistant is a phone-based personal assistant service that helps users manage travel bookings, calendar events, reminders, and email — all through a single voice call. Atlas handles multi-step requests and keeps track of everything discussed during the conversation.

## Service Hours
- Monday–Friday: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Pacific Time)
- Saturday–Sunday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Pacific Time)
- Holidays: Limited hours (9:00 AM – 5:00 PM)

## Contact
- **Phone:** 1-800-ATLAS-PA (1-800-285-2772)
- **Email:** support@atlaspa.com

## Reminders

Atlas can set date-and-time reminders that are delivered as push notifications to the user's phone. Reminders require a date, time, and message.

---

## Policies & Limitations
- Atlas cannot make purchases over $1,000 without verbal confirmation and a confirmation code sent via SMS.
- Flight bookings are held for 24 hours before payment is processed.
- Calendar events can only be modified for the user's own calendar (no shared calendar editing).
- Emails longer than 500 words require the user's approval before sending.
- Atlas does not store credit card information between sessions.
