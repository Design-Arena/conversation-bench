
# Harvest & Hearth Market

## Overview
Harvest & Hearth Market is a specialty grocery and artisan bakery in Pasadena, California. We take phone orders for delivery and in-store pickup. We carry locally sourced produce, baked goods, pantry staples, and gourmet specialty items.

## Confusable Items Reference
The following items are commonly confused by customers on the phone:
- **Flour vs Flower:** All-Purpose Flour (#1010, $6.99) vs Fresh Flower Bouquet (#8010, $34.99) — homophones
- **Gray vs Grey:** Gray Sea Salt (#3010, $8.49) vs Grey Goose Cheese (#2025, $12.99) — spelling/sound
- **Sourdough Loaf vs Rolls:** Sourdough Loaf (#5010, $7.50) vs Sourdough Rolls (#5015, $5.50) — same product type
- **Honey Crisp vs Honey Graham:** Honey Crisp Apples (#6010, $3.99/lb) vs Honey Graham Crackers (#7010, $4.99/box) — "honey" prefix
- **Italian Parsley vs Italian Seasoning:** Italian Parsley (#6020, $2.49/bunch) vs Italian Seasoning (#3020, $5.99/jar) — "Italian" prefix
- **Organic Eggs vs Organic Egg Noodles:** Organic Eggs (#2015, $6.49/dz) vs Organic Egg Noodles (#7015, $4.29/bag) — "organic egg" prefix
- **Item #1330 vs #1303:** Maple Syrup (#1330, $9.99) vs Maple Candy (#1303, $13.99) — transposed digits
- **Item #4015 vs #4051:** Olive Oil (#4015, $12.99) vs Kalamata Olives (#4051, $8.49) — transposed digits

## Delivery Information

### Delivery Zones & Fees
- **Zone 1 (within 5 miles of store):** $5.99 delivery fee
- **Zone 2 (5–10 miles):** $8.99 delivery fee
- **Zone 3 (10–15 miles):** $12.99 delivery fee
- Orders over $75 qualify for **free delivery** in Zone 1.
- We do not deliver beyond 15 miles.

### Delivery Times
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM are eligible for same-day delivery (subject to availability).
- Standard delivery window: 2–4 hours from order confirmation.
- Customers may request a preferred delivery window (morning, afternoon, or evening).

### Minimum Order
- Minimum order for delivery: $25.00
- No minimum for in-store pickup.

## Payment Accepted
- Cash (delivery drivers carry change for up to $50)
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- Apple Pay, Google Pay (in-store and online)
- Store gift cards
- Payment is collected at time of order for card payments, or upon delivery for cash.

## Return & Exchange Policy
- **Perishable items** (produce, dairy, bakery): Must report issues within 24 hours of delivery. We will issue a full refund or replacement.
- **Non-perishable items:** May be returned within 7 days with receipt for a full refund or exchange.
- **Damaged or incorrect items:** Contact us immediately. We will send a replacement at no extra charge or issue a refund.
- **No returns** on floral items once delivered, unless damaged in transit.
