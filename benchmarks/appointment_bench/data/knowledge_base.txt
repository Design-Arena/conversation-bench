
# Bayshore Family Dental

## Overview
Bayshore Family Dental is a full-service dental practice located in San Francisco, California.
We offer general dentistry, orthodontics, and oral surgery services for patients of all ages.

## Services & Durations

### General Dentistry (Dr. Perry)
- **Standard Cleaning:** 45 minutes — Professional teeth cleaning, polish, and fluoride treatment.
- **Comprehensive Exam / Checkup:** 30 minutes — Full oral exam, X-rays if needed, treatment planning.
- **Filling (composite):** 60 minutes — Tooth-colored composite resin filling for cavities.
- **Crown Preparation:** 90 minutes — Preparation and temporary crown placement; permanent crown fitted at follow-up visit.
- **Teeth Whitening (in-office):** 60 minutes — Professional-grade whitening treatment.

### Orthodontics (Dr. Barry)
- **Orthodontic Consultation:** 45 minutes — Assessment, X-rays, and treatment plan discussion.
- **Braces Adjustment:** 30 minutes — Routine tightening and progress check.
- **Invisalign Check-in:** 20 minutes — Progress review and new aligner fitting.

### Oral Surgery (Dr. Patel)
- **Simple Extraction:** 45 minutes — Removal of a single tooth under local anesthesia.
- **Wisdom Teeth Removal:** 90 minutes — Surgical extraction of one or more wisdom teeth.
- **Dental Implant Placement:** 120 minutes — Surgical placement of a titanium implant post.

## Appointment Policies
- **Cancellation policy:** Please provide at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a $50 fee.
- **First-time patients:** Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment to complete intake paperwork. You can also download forms from our website ahead of time.
- **Appointment reminders:** We send text and email reminders 48 hours and 24 hours before your appointment.
- **Emergency appointments:** Same-day emergency slots are available on a limited basis. Call our office directly for emergencies.

## Standard Appointment Slots
